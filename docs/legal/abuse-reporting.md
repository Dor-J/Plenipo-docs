# Abuse Reporting

Report abuse to `abuse@plenipo.dev`.

Include as much of the following as possible:

- Reporter contact.
- DID(s), registry route URLs, envelope IDs, payment IDs, invoice numbers, timestamps, or IPs when available.
- Description of the behavior.
- Evidence you are authorized to disclose. Do not send private keys, API keys, sidecar bearer tokens, or secrets.

Plenipo may respond by rate limiting, suspending a DID, delisting a Registry record, preserving relevant records, or requesting more information. Message plaintext is normally unavailable to Plenipo unless a reporter intentionally provides plaintext they control.

Normal reports are triaged within one business day. Active exploitation or safety-critical reports are triaged as soon as possible.
