# Lawful Requests

Lawful requests should be sent to `legal@plenipo.dev`.

Requests must identify:

- Legal authority and jurisdiction.
- Requested DIDs, accounts, registry records, envelope IDs, payment references, or time range.
- Contact information for the requesting authority.
- Any preservation or nondisclosure requirement.

Plenipo can generally produce retained routing metadata, audit events, auth/session metadata, registry metadata, billing records, and operational logs. Plenipo Core and Registry generally cannot produce message plaintext, user private keys, or message decryption keys because they do not possess them.

Plenipo reviews requests for validity, scope, and legal constraints. When legally allowed, affected operators may be notified.
